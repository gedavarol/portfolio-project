Cappadocia Balloon Tour Project

I started to my coding journey with this project. The project is about joining the balloon tour during sunset in beautiful Cappadocia, Turkey. On VS Code, I built a simple page using HTML, CSS and thanks to JavaScript, the Join Balloon Tour button was enabled. After 3-week of SheCodes Basic course, I deserved to get the certificate of "Introduction to Coding" which is published on my LinkedIn profile.

To apply the balloon tour, you just need to answer some questions. The criteria here is the age limit. People who are less than 16 years old are not allowed to join this tour without their parent`s permissions. When you are old enough (equal or more than 16 years), the page is asking for your email to send more information (in the real tour case of course). I added some information about Cappadocia as well.
